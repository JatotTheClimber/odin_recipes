Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all baked until golden and bubbling.

Often seasoned with garlic, herbs, and tomato, lasagna is a hearty, comforting meal that blends savory flavors and textures in every bite. Variations can include vegetables, different meats, or cheeses, making it a versatile favorite around the world.
