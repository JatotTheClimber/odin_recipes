Japanese Gyoza

Description

Some little meaty potstickers!

Japanese dumplings that are small and tasty

Ingredients

  • ground pork
  • dumpling wrappers
  • canola oil
  • water

Steps

  1. Put the ground pork in the dumpling wrappers
  2. Heat up oil in a pan
  3. Fry the dumplings in the pan fat side down
  4. steam the dumplings in the same pan with the oil then serve
